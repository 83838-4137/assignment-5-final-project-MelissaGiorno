Group Parties & Reservations

Our private dining experience.
Book us for your next event.
We offer private dining, catering, and bar services.

  • Meriggiare accepts group reservations of up to 18 guests.

  • For parties of 19 to our maximum capacity of 42 guests, only private buyouts are available.

  • Due to the restaurants size, for groups of 9 or more guests
    will require a deposit to hold the reservation. The deposit
    will go towards the final bill and is fully refundable if
    cancellation is made 5 days prior to the booked event.
